# WWF NbS Virtual Assistant — Proof of Concept - Version 2 with questionnaire
Overview

This project is a proof-of-concept decision-support platform that demonstrates how WWF’s Nature-based Solutions (NbS) portfolio could be structured into a scalable, corporate-facing digital product aligned with SBTN (Science Based Targets for Nature).

The prototype simulates how a company’s geographic exposure and environmental risk profile can be translated into:

Prioritized NbS investment opportunities

Ranked project recommendations

Measurable impact outcomes

SBTN-ready reporting language

The data used in this demo is mock and structured for demonstration purposes only.

# Strategic Objective

The objective of this prototype is to demonstrate how:

Corporate exposure + basin-level environmental risk + intervention logic + project metrics
can be integrated into a structured, transparent recommendation engine.

This addresses key market challenges:

High transaction costs in NbS sourcing

Fragmented environmental risk data

Difficulty translating project metrics into SBTN-aligned reporting

Limited visibility into intervention suitability

The prototype illustrates how WWF assets could be productized into a digital decision-support platform.

Impact Metrics
        ↓

        ↓

# Business Implication

This proof of concept demonstrates how WWF could:

Productize its NbS portfolio into a scalable digital platform

Reduce due diligence and screening costs for corporates

Enable structured SBTN-aligned reporting

Increase transparency and trust in NbS selection

It showcases a shift from advisory-based engagement to platform-enabled sustainability services.
